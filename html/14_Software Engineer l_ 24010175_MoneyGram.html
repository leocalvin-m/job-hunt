Software Engineer l- 24010175

Description & Requirements

The Software Engineer I performs low complexity software development tasks related to both supporting or enhancing existing software applications/systems and creating new software applications/systems; the tasks are performed under constant supervision. The Software Engineer I is familiar with basic software development concepts, practices and procedures, able to apply them and learn more complex concepts. The tasks performed by the Software Engineer I may be similar to those performed by the Software Engineer II, but are performed with much more direction and assistance from a higher level position.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Performs clearly defined software development tasks of low complexity.
  • Participates in developing fixes or enhancements in existing applications/systems and/or new applications as specified.
  • Understands and uses technical documentation.
  • May perform system testing and/or other dutes as assigned.

Qualifications Education

Bachelor of Science in computer science or a related technical field, or equivalent combination of post-secondary education and work experience.

Experience

1+ year of study in the field of software engineering or equivalent.

Essential Skills

  • Ability to learn.
  • Demonstrated ability to gather requirements, design and develop systems.
  • Team player and individual contributor.
  • Ability to accurately identify root cause of technical problems.
  • Excellent communication skills; verbal and written.
  • Ability to adapt to change.
