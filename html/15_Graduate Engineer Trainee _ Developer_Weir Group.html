Graduate Engineer Trainee – Developer Division: WeirGroup Location

Bangalore(Hybrid)

Purpose of Role: Role: As a developer, your role involves translating business needs into functional software solutions. Key Tasks: You’ll handle technical implementation, problem-solving, efficiency, user experience, and security. Purpose: Your purpose is to contribute to digital project success, learn and grow, collaborate with the team, ensure quality, and continuously improve. Impact: Embrace the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on digital projects. Teamwork: Collaborate with designers, product managers, and other team members for cohesive solutions.

Why choose Weir:

We continually focus on people and their wellbeing. We believe in fairness and choose to be honest, transparent and authentic in everything we do.

Key Responsibilities: Understand Business Requirements:* As a fresher, your role involves comprehending business needs and translating them into functional software solutions. You’ll collaborate with stakeholders to ensure the software aligns with their requirements.

Apply Technical Methods:* You’ll apply statistical and machine learning techniques to extract insights from data. Additionally, you’ll generate reports and dashboards to communicate these findings effectively.

Develop and Deploy Solutions:* Your responsibility includes developing, testing, and deploying data solutions and applications. Whether it’s using .NET, React, or JavaScript, you’ll contribute to building reliable software.

Effective Communication:* As a fresher, you’ll communicate data findings and recommendations to stakeholders and clients. Clear communication is essential for successful project outcomes.

Continuous Learning: Stay updated with new data technologies and industry best practices. Your commitment to learning will enhance your skills and contribute to project success.

Safety First: Demonstrate 100% commitment to our zero harm behaviors in support of our drive towards developing a world class safety culture.

Job Knowledge/Education and Qualifications:* Bachelor's degree or higher in computer science, IS/IT. * At least one year of experience in Frontend/backend development tools or technologies. * Proficient in .net, SQL, and React/Javascript frameworks * Familiar with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud. * Knowledge of Software Developemnt Lifecycle. * Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and curiosity. * Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written. * Ability to work independently and in a team environment.

Objectives and measurements Technical Proficiency:* Objective: Gain proficiency in relevant programming languages, frameworks, and tools. * KPIs: + Completion of assigned coding tasks within specified timeframes. + Active participation in code reviews and feedback incorporation.

Problem-Solving Skills:* Objective: Develop the ability to analyze and solve technical challenges. * KPIs: + Successfully troubleshoot and resolve coding issues. + Propose innovative solutions to enhance project functionality.

Collaboration and Communication:* Objective: Work effectively within a team environment. * KPIs: + Regularly participate in team meetings and discussions. + Provide clear and concise updates on progress.

Quality Code Delivery:* Objective: Deliver clean, maintainable, and efficient code. * KPIs: + Maintain a low defect rate (minimal bugs). + Adherence to coding standards and best practices.

Learning and Growth:* Objective: Continuously learn and improve skills. * KPIs: + Completion of online courses or certifications. + Active participation in knowledge-sharing sessions.
