Position Summary

Power BI Developer

Description & Requirements

Job Summary

Power BI developers are responsible for the development and administration of BI tools. They need to have complete knowledge of the Power BI system. The main duty of Power BI developers is to convene raw data into meaningful analysis with the help of interactive and easy to understand dashboards and reports.

Experience

3+ Years

Location

Mumbai

Job Category

Full Time Job, IT, Cloud engineer

Job Description

Responsibilities

1.Power BI development and administration.

  1. Building Analysis Services reporting models.

  2. Developing visual reports, dashboards and KPI scorecards using Power BI desktop.

  3. Connecting to data sources, importing data, and transforming data for Business Intelligence.

  4. Excellent in analytical thinking for translating data into info native visuals and reports.

  5. Able to implement row level security on data and have an understanding of application security layer models in Power BI

  6. Expertise in using advance level calculations on the data set.

  7. Responsible for design methodology and project documentation.

  8. Able to develop tabular and multidimensional models that are compatible with warehouse standards.

  9. Adept in developing, publishing and scheduling Power BI replots as per the business Requirements.

  10. Able to properly understand the business requirements and develop data models accordingly by taking care of the resources.

Requirements

  • Development experience in Microsoft 365 technologies including SharePoint Online and the Power Platform (PowerApps, Power Automate, Power BI)
  • SharePoint/Power Platform related certification
  • Troubleshooting abilities
  • Ability to learn new emerging technologies.
  • Excellent communication skills (written as well as Verbal)

Qualification

  • Min 3+ years of experience with respect to Microsoft 365 technologies.
  • Good knowledge of Power Platform and related emerging Technologies.
  • BE, BSc in Computer Science, or relevant field.

Microsoft Certifications

SharePoint related certifications and PL-900 would be an added advantage.

Certification is not mandatory, and TMTPL can provide training for the same.
