React Native Developer

Description & Requirements

Job Summary

Softfruit Solutions is looking for a React Native developer having good knowledge on mobile app development. The ideal candidate will be a part of a full-stack creative team that is responsible for all aspects of the ongoing development of our sports related platform - Playspots.

Required Skills :

  • Proven experience as a React Native Developer with a strong portfolio showcasing mobile applications you've developed.
  • In-depth knowledge of the React Native framework, JavaScript, and mobile app development best practices.
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs, third-party libraries, and integration of external services.

Responsibilities and Duties

  • Working on the sports related booking platform ( Playspots App ) to enhance the performance and User experience of the applications
  • Work in an Agile environment with Daily Stand-ups
  • Coding/Debugging Android and iOS apps.
  • Collaborate with designers to build pixel-perfect, detail-oriented interfaces
  • Ability to work with Git
  • Work with the team to manage, maintain code and do tests to ensure the product is of the highest quality.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
  • Minimum 1 years of professional experience in mobile app development
  • Strong portfolio of successfully launched applications on the App Store and Google Play Store
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment

Freshers can also apply ( Those who are completed internship in React native)

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: ₹20,000.00 - ₹40,000.00 per month

Schedule:

  • Day shift

Education:

  • Bachelor's (Preferred)

Experience:

  • Mobile applications: 1 year (Required)

Work Location: In person
