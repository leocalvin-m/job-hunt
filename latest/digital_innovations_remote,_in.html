Remote Figma UI Designer (Contract - 1 Month)

  • Strong understanding of design principles such as hierarchy, balance, color theory, and typography.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to work independently and manage time effectively.
  • A portfolio showcasing your UI design work for web and mobile applications.
