RPA Developer

Job Summary:
What you will do:
  • Work with business analysts and process owners to understand the business requirements and specifications for the automation solutions.
  • Design, develop, and deploy automation scripts, workflows, and bots using RPA tools and technologies such as UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, etc.
  • Follow best practices and standards for RPA development, such as naming conventions, code reusability, modularity, exception handling, logging, etc.
  • • Perform testing and debugging of the automation solutions to ensure quality and functionality • Troubleshoot and resolve any issues or errors related to the automation solutions.
  • Maintain and update the automation solutions as per the changing business needs and requirements.
  • • Document the automation solutions and provide technical support and guidance to end users • Monitor and measure the performance and benefits of the automation solutions, with clearly defined KPIs on value delivered.
  • Continuously research and learn new RPA tools and technologies to improve the automation capabilities.
What you will need:
Education:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field **Experience:**
  • A minimum of 3-5 years of experience as a Developer or in a similar role, preferably working on RPA projects.
  • Proficiency in developing automation scripts, workflows, and bots using RPA tools and technologies such as UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, etc.
  • Knowledge of AI/ML frameworks and platforms such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Azure Cognitive Services, Databricks, etc.
  • Experience in working with various applications and systems such as web, desktop, mainframe, SAP, etc.
  • Experience in conducting testing and debugging of automation solutions. **Skills:**
  • Strong knowledge of programming languages such as C#, VB.NET, Python, Java, etc.
  • Strong knowledge of RPA tools and technologies such as UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Blue Prism, etc.
  • Strong grasp of software development life cycle (SDLC) methodologies such as Agile, Scrum, etc.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, critical thinking, and decision-making skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
  • Effective time management and task prioritization skills.
  • A keen eye for detail and a commitment to delivering high-quality work.
