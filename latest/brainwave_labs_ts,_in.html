Game Developer

Join the Game Development Revolution at Brainwave Labs!**
Company Overview:**
Position Available:** Game Developer
Responsibilities:
  • Lead AR/VR Game Development: Drive the creation of immersive gaming experiences from concept to deployment using AR/VR technologies.
  • Collaborate with Cross-functional Teams: Work closely with artists, designers, engineers, and QA testers to integrate AR/VR features seamlessly into game projects.
  • Prototype and Experiment: Explore new ideas and technologies to enhance user experiences in AR/VR environments. Rapidly prototype concepts and iterate based on feedback.
  • Optimize Performance: Implement optimizations to ensure smooth performance and frame rates in AR/VR applications across various platforms.
  • Stay Updated with Industry Trends: Keep abreast of the latest advancements in AR/VR technologies and gaming trends. Apply new techniques and best practices to improve game development processes.
  • Code Review and Mentorship: Conduct code reviews to maintain code quality and provide mentorship to junior developers. Share knowledge and expertise with team members to foster continuous learning and growth.
  • Problem Solving: Identify and address technical challenges and bottlenecks in AR/VR development. Implement creative solutions to enhance user engagement and interactivity.
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field. Master's degree preferred.
  • Extensive experience in game development with a focus on AR/VR technologies.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as C#, C++, or UnityScript.
  • Experience with Unity or Unreal Engine is highly desirable. Strong understanding of 3D graphics, physics, and mathematics as they relate to game development.
  • Previous experience with ARKit, ARCore, Oculus SDK, or other AR/VR development frameworks.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills with the ability to work well within a cross-functional team.
  • A passion for gaming and a keen interest in exploring the potential of AR/VR technologies to create groundbreaking experiences.
